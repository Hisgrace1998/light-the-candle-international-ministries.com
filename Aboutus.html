About Us

Our ministry is a rising global ministry aimed at reaching out to people through evangelism and discipleship. We aim to make people good citizens who love God as their hunger for Him increases.

The ministry is located in Mbarara district in Western Uganda and is open to all groups of people who desire to know more about God. We have partnered with various ministries across the world, both financially and spiritually, to achieve Godly objectives.

“You are the light of the world. A town built on a hill cannot be hidden.” – Matthew 5:14

“Go therefore and make disciples of all nations.” – Matthew 28:19

Our Features

  • Global partnerships for evangelism and discipleship.
  • Community outreach programs and spiritual growth initiatives.
  • Inclusive approach to all groups of people.
  • Mission-focused on spreading God’s love worldwide.
